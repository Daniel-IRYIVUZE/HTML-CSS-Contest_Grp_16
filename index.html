    Connecting students with mentors for career development

    CareerAid aims to bridge the gap between university students and industry professionals by providing a platform for mentorship and career development. Our primary objective is to empower students to set and achieve their career goals by connecting them with experienced mentors who can offer valuable guidance, advice, and support. Through CareerAid, students can gain insights into various career paths, receive personalized advice on resume building and job searching, and establish meaningful connections with professionals in their field of interest.

    About Us


    Our website's key objective is to create a seamless and user friendly experience for both students and mentors. By offering a categorized list of mentors and their expertise, we aim to make it easy for students to find mentors who align with their career aspirations. Additionally, our platform serves as a resource hub where students can access valuable careerrelated resources and tools to enhance their professional development journey. Ultimately, CareerAid strives to foster a supportive community where students can thrive and succeed in their future careers.
